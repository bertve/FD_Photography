About me

Hi, my name is Frederic D’hondt, I’m a 20-years old student based in Ghent. I’m very passionate about Photography, and the things I like are bumping myself into countries to capture all the stunning places and habits. When I’m not home, you’ll most likely find me exploring nature or cities.

My passion started in April 2018. I had 0% experience with photography, but then I decided to plan a road trip to Germany with 4 friends who already were very into photography. Quickly, I became involved with them. So, from that moment on, my new passion raised. Nowadays, I’m capturing all my special travel moments.

The thing that inspires me the most, is walking or driving into infinity, just to see that one extraordinary view. When I am on the road, I barely ever sleep, because I live my days from sunrise till night. Photography gives me the power to add value to myself. As a result, escaping the everyday life and traveling are my priority affairs.
