About me

Hi, My name is Frederic D’hondt, I’m a 20-years old student based in Ghent, I’m very passionate about Photography and I like to bump myself into countries to capture all the stunning places and habits. When I’m not home you’ll most likely find me exploring the nature or the city centers.

My passion started in April 2018. My experience with photography was 0 %. But then I decided to plan a road trip to Germany with 4 friends who already were very into photography. Quickly, I became involved with them. So from that point my new passion raised. Nowadays, I’m capturing all my special travel moments.

The thing that inspires me the most is walking or driving into endless to see that massive view. When I’m on the road I barely never sleep because I live my days from sunrise to night. Photography gives me the power to add value for myself. Escape the everyday life and travel are my priority affaires.
